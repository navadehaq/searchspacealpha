How SearchSpace Works

A deterministic, benchmark-driven pipeline that turns real performance into real opportunities — no resumes, no gatekeeping.

For Competitors

Prove it. Get hired.

Skip the resume screen. Every competition on SearchSpace is scored against a fixed, published benchmark — your rank is your résumé.

  1. Pick a competition

    Browse live data science, ML, and algorithmic challenges posted directly by hiring companies.

  2. Build your solution

    Download the dataset, read the scoring rules, and submit your model or approach before the deadline.

  3. Get ranked automatically

    Submissions are scored against a held-out benchmark. No panel opinions, no bias — just the numbers.

  4. Win prizes and interviews

    Top finishers split the cash prize pool and receive guaranteed interviews with the hiring company.

For Companies

Hire on signal, not credentials.

Replace slow, noisy screening funnels with a single benchmark that surfaces your best candidates — and generates real work product along the way.

  1. Define the benchmark

    Work with our team to translate a real problem from your roadmap into a dataset and a scoring metric.

  2. Set the prize structure

    Choose your cash prize pool and how many guaranteed interview slots to offer top performers.

  3. Launch the competition

    SearchSpace hosts the competition page, dataset access, and anonymized submission pipeline.

  4. Interview the leaderboard

    Skip to final-round interviews with candidates who've already demonstrated the exact skills you need.
