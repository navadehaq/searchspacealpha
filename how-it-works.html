How SearchSpace Works

A competition-driven hiring platform for tech companies

For Competitors

Solve problems, get hired.

Your score replaces your resume. No AI or ATS involved.

  1. Pick a competition

    Browse live data science, ML, and algorithmic challenges posted by companies seeking candidates.

  2. Build your solution

    Download the dataset, understand the guidelines, and submit your solution before the deadline.

  3. Get ranked automatically

    Submissions are scored and ranked against a benchmark. No AI, ATS, or bias, just a single deterministic metric.

  4. Get hired and paid

    Top finishers receive guaranteed interviews with the hiring company, plus a share of the prize pool.

For Companies

Find the top 1% talent.

Replace ineffective hiring processes. See who can do real work.

  1. Define the competition

    Propose a problem. Our team will work with you to craft a competition designed to evaluate applicants.

  2. Launch the competition

    SearchSpace will host the competition page, featuring the guidelines, data, and submission pipeline.

  3. Analyze the submissions

    You will receive a candidate ranking after our in-house infrastructure processes all submissions.

  4. Interview top performers

    Based on the ranking, interview top performers of your SearchSpace competition and identify your next hire.
