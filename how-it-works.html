How SearchSpace Works

A competition-driven hiring platform for tech companies

For Competitors

Solve problems, get hired.

Skip the broken screening process. Your score is your resume.

  1. Pick a competition

    Browse live data science, ML, and algorithmic challenges posted by companies seeking candidates.

  2. Build your solution

    Download the dataset, understand the guidelines, and submit your solution before the deadline.

  3. Get ranked automatically

    Submissions are deterministically scored against a benchmark. No AI, ATS, or bias, just raw performance.

  4. Get hired and paid

    Top finishers receive guaranteed interviews with the hiring company, plus a share of the prize pool.

For Companies

Find the top 1% talent.

Replace ineffective hiring processes. See who can do real work.

  1. Define the competition

    Work with our team to translate a real problem from your company into a competition.

  2. Set the prize structure

    Choose your cash prize pool and guaranteed interview slots to offer top performers.

  3. Launch the competition

    SearchSpace hosts the competition page, dataset access, and submission pipeline.

  4. Interview top performers

    Skip to final-round interviews with top-performing candidates, leaving the heavy lifting to us.
